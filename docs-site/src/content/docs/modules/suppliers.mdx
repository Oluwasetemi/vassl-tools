The Suppliers module stores contact information for companies and individuals that Video Access Security Solutions Ltd procures from. Suppliers can be assigned as the **preferred supplier** on individual products.

Open with **Cmd+4** (macOS) / **Ctrl+4** (Windows).

## Supplier list

The panel shows all suppliers with:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Column</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Name</td><td>Supplier company or individual name</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Contact Person</td><td>Primary contact</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Email</td><td>Contact email</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Phone</td><td>Contact phone number</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

### Filtering

Use the search bar to filter by supplier name in real time.

## Adding a supplier

<Steps>
1. Press **Cmd+N** (macOS) / **Ctrl+N** (Windows) or click **+ New Supplier**.
2. Fill in the supplier form:

   <table>
     <thead><tr><th>Field</th><th>Required</th><th>Notes</th></tr></thead>
     <tbody>
       <tr><td>Name</td><td>Yes</td><td>Must be unique across all suppliers</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Contact Person</td><td>No</td><td>Primary contact individual at the supplier</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Email</td><td>No</td><td>Contact email address</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Phone</td><td>No</td><td>Contact phone number</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Notes</td><td>No</td><td>Any additional notes about this supplier</td></tr>
     </tbody>
   </table>

3. Save.
</Steps>

## Preferred suppliers

Each product in the Inventory can have a **preferred supplier** assigned. This is used to:

- Pre-fill the supplier field when adding stock entries
- Indicate the primary source for re-ordering

To assign a preferred supplier:

1. Open the Inventory module.
2. Edit a product.
3. Select a supplier from the **Preferred Supplier** dropdown.

<Aside type="note">
Preferred supplier is informational only — you can still add stock from any supplier when creating a stock entry.
</Aside>
