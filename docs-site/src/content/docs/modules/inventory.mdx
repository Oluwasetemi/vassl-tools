The Inventory module is the product catalogue for Video Access Security Solutions Ltd. It tracks what products exist, how much stock is on hand, and whether stock is above or below minimum levels.

Open with **Cmd+1** (macOS) / **Ctrl+1** (Windows).

## Product list

The main panel shows all products as a table with:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Column</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>SKU</td><td>Unique product code</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Name</td><td>Product display name</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Category</td><td>Optional grouping (e.g. Cameras, Networking)</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Unit</td><td>Unit of measure (pcs, box, roll, etc.)</td></tr>
    <tr><td>In Stock</td><td>Current total quantity across all stock entries</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Min Level</td><td>Minimum acceptable stock quantity</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Status</td><td>Green = healthy · Amber = low · Red = critical</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

### Stock status colours

Stock status is calculated from the current quantity relative to the minimum stock level:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Colour</th><th>Status</th><th>Condition</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Green</td><td>Healthy</td><td>Stock &gt; Min × 1.2</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Amber</td><td>Low</td><td>Min &lt; Stock ≤ Min × 1.2</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Red</td><td>Critical</td><td>Stock ≤ Min (and Min &gt; 0)</td></tr>
    <tr><td>—</td><td>No alert</td><td>Min stock level = 0</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

The 20 % buffer above minimum (the Amber zone) gives advance warning before stock actually hits the critical threshold.

<Aside type="note">
Products with a Min Stock Level of 0 show no status badge — they are considered untracked for restock purposes.
</Aside>

### Filtering

Use the search bar at the top of the panel (`Cmd+F` / `Ctrl+F`) to filter products by name, SKU, or category in real time. Press the **×** button to clear the filter.

## Adding a product

<Steps>
1. Press **Cmd+N** (macOS) / **Ctrl+N** (Windows) or click **+ New Product**.
2. Fill in the product form:

   <table>
     <thead><tr><th>Field</th><th>Required</th><th>Notes</th></tr></thead>
     <tbody>
       <tr><td>SKU</td><td>Yes</td><td>Unique across all products. Immutable after creation.</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Name</td><td>Yes</td><td>Display name shown in lists and quotations</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Category</td><td>No</td><td>Optional grouping (e.g. Cameras, Networking)</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Unit</td><td>Yes</td><td>Unit of measure — e.g. <code>pcs</code>, <code>box</code>, <code>roll</code>, <code>metre</code></td></tr>
       <tr><td>Model #</td><td>No</td><td>Manufacturer model number</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Part #</td><td>No</td><td>Manufacturer part number</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Duty %</td><td>No</td><td>Import duty percentage applied in price calculations (default 0)</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Initial Stock</td><td>No</td><td>Starting quantity on hand (create mode only)</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Min Stock Level</td><td>No</td><td>Threshold for restock alerts (default 0)</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Description</td><td>No</td><td>Longer product notes or specifications</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Preferred Supplier</td><td>No</td><td>Select from your supplier list</td></tr>
     </tbody>
   </table>

3. Press **Enter** or click **Save**.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
SKU is immutable after creation. Choose a consistent format — for example `HIK-DS2CD2143G2` for Hikvision model DS2CD2143G2, or `CAM-IP-2MP` for a generic 2 MP IP camera.
</Aside>

### Editing a product

Right-click any product row and select **Edit Product**. In edit mode:

- **SKU** is shown as a read-only label (cannot be changed after creation)
- **Current Stock** is editable — changing this value creates an automatic **Adjustment** stock entry for the delta
- All other fields behave the same as in create mode

## Adding stock (New Entry)

Stock is added through **stock entries** — each entry records a delivery or acquisition event.

<Steps>
1. Select a product row in the list.
2. Click **+ New Entry** (enabled when a product is selected).
3. Fill in the stock entry form:

   <table>
     <thead><tr><th>Field</th><th>Required</th><th>Notes</th></tr></thead>
     <tbody>
       <tr><td>Quantity</td><td>Yes</td><td>Units received (can be negative for Adjustments)</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Unit Cost (USD)</td><td>Yes</td><td>Cost per unit at time of receipt</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Supplier</td><td>No</td><td>Who supplied this batch</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Acquisition Type</td><td>Yes</td><td><code>Restock</code> · <code>Project</code> · <code>Adjustment</code></td></tr>
       <tr><td>Acquired At</td><td>Yes</td><td>Date received</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Invoice Ref</td><td>No</td><td>Invoice or purchase order reference</td></tr>
       <tr><td>Notes</td><td>No</td><td>Any additional notes</td></tr>
     </tbody>
   </table>

4. Save. The product's **In Stock** count updates immediately.
</Steps>

**Acquisition types explained:**

<table>
  <thead><tr><th>Type</th><th>When to use</th></tr></thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td><code>Restock</code></td><td>Standard purchase from a supplier</td></tr>
    <tr><td><code>Project</code></td><td>Stock received for a specific project</td></tr>
    <tr><td><code>Adjustment</code></td><td>Manual correction — use a negative quantity to remove stock</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Restock Alerts tab

Switch to the **Restock Alerts** tab (next to Products) to see only the products that are in **Critical** or **Low** status. This is a filtered view — no separate data.

## Context menu

Right-click any product row for quick actions:

- **Edit Product** — open the edit form
- **Price History** — jump to that product's price history in the Price Book
- **Add Price Entry** — open the Price Book entry form for that product
